Who We Are

We undertake Turnkey contracts for liquefied, compressed, flammable, corrosive, toxic liquids, and gaseous storage handling systems covered under SMPV (U) Rules 2013, as well as bottling systems under Cylinder rules, including FUEL, Propane/LPG, etc. Comprised of an advanced team of technicians, along with a highly developed office staff, Durexa Engineering LLP is a local Propane/LPG provider located in Morbi, India, equipped with specialized knowledge and abundant resources.

Our team members have 22 years of working experience in the oil and gas industry in India, with a track record of holding responsible senior management positions in Propane installations at Durexa Engineering LLP. With deep roots in the industry and expertise in advanced propane technologies, we are well-equipped to provide our customers with the absolute best service at the most competitive prices.
