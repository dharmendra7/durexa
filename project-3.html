LPG/PROPANE CYLINDER FILLING PLANT INSTALLATION:

LPG/PROPANE Cylinder filling plant is also known as LPG/PROPANE bottling plant. It can be installed in very small scale like in skid mode / form and also with very huge capacity of bulk storage.

Capacity of the storage and machinery is depending on the Cylinder filling demand.

Cylinder filling Skid can be design and manufacture form 1 scale to 8 scales according to the requirements. Main point of this type of system is to locate all equipment’s on a skid system and it could be tailor made regarding to the countries’ regulations and work environment.

What are the Equipment of LPG/PROPANE cylinder filling plants?

  • LPG/PROPANE Electronic Scale with multiple Filling Heads up to + 50 kg
  • LPG/PROPANE Mechanical Scale with multiple Filling Heads up to + 50 kg
  • LPG/PROPANE Cylinder Filling Carousel
  • Telescopic Conveyor
  • Chain Conveyor
  • Washing Cabinet
  • Drying Machine
  • Carousel Entry Mechanism
  • Belt Conveyor
  • LPG/PROPANE Cylinder Weight Check Scale
  • LPG/PROPANE Cylinder Leakage Check Pool
  • LPG/PROPANE Cylinder valve Shrinking Machine
  • Valve screwing & unscrewing unit
  • Gas Transfer Unit
  • Hydrostatic Cylinder Test Unit
  • Air and LPG/PROPANE Hubs of Filling Carousel

Types of Cylinder filling Plants:

  • Skid mounted mini cylinder filling plant
  • Containerised cylinder filling plant
  • Manual Operated filling plant
  • Automatic Filling Carrousels
